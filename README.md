## üéØ **Problem Statement**
Shoppers face three key challenges in online fashion:  
- **Lack of peer influence** ‚Äì friends‚Äô opinions are missing in product discovery.  
- **Overwhelming reviews** ‚Äì customers struggle to extract useful insights.  
- **Decision fatigue** ‚Äì comparing wishlist items is tedious and leads to drop-offs.  

---

## üöÄ **Our Solution ‚Äì 3 Features**

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ **Social Circle Recommendations**
- **What it is**: Hyper-personalised product feed powered by a user‚Äôs close connections.  
- **How it works**: Builds a **social graph** from selected friends (min. 10‚Äì20 for privacy), merges it with **browsing history & style preferences**, and shows **socially validated recommendations**.  
- **Impact**: Boosts trust, improves discovery, and increases CTR & conversions.  

**Tech Used & Why**  
- **SentenceTransformers** ‚Üí generate embeddings for similarity between users & products.  
- **CLIP** ‚Üí align product images with textual metadata for richer matching.
- **BART**‚Üí sequence-to-sequence model designed for both understanding and text generation, so as to sort out the similar items for recommendation. 
- **MongoDB (Friendship Graph)** ‚Üí store user-friend connections securely.

### 2Ô∏è‚É£ **AI Review Summarization**
- **What it is**: An AI component that condenses hundreds of reviews into a simple **‚ÄúLoved vs Found Issues‚Äù** summary.  
- **How it works**: Uses **BERT / FLAN-T5** for summarization + **sentiment analysis** to highlight top positives & negatives.  
- **Impact**: Reduces decision uncertainty, builds confidence, and shortens product evaluation time.  

**Tech Used & Why**  
- **BERT** ‚Üí strong at sentiment classification (positive, neutral, negative).  
- **FLAN-T5** ‚Üí optimized for **short, human-readable summaries**.  
- **MongoDB (Reviews Collection)** ‚Üí store review text and embeddings for retrieval.

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ **Wishlist Contrast Engine**
- **What it is**: A smart **comparison assistant** for wishlist items.  
- **How it works**: Uses **embeddings & contextual recommendation** to auto-compare items (price, fit, delivery, reviews) and highlight best-value picks.  
- **Impact**: Speeds up decision-making, lowers cart drop-offs, and drives conversions.  

**Tech Used & Why**  
-**FLAN-T5**‚Üí summarisation of the comparison of the products suggested by AI.
- **Custom Embeddings** ‚Üí represent product attributes for better contextual comparisons.  
- **Hybrid Scoring (Rules + ML)** ‚Üí combine business logic (price, delivery) with AI (preferences).  
- **React (UI)** ‚Üí dynamic comparison modal with highlights of best picks.
